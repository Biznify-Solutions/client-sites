Frequently Asked Questions

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies depending on the scope and complexity of your project. We provide free, detailed estimates for all services with no hidden fees. Our transparent pricing ensures you know exactly what you're paying for before work begins.

How long will my project take to complete?

Project timelines depend on the specific work involved. Small repairs may be completed the same day, while larger renovations could take several weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your estimate and keep you updated throughout the process.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured for your protection and peace of mind. Our insurance covers both liability and workers' compensation, and we're happy to provide proof of insurance before beginning any work.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Absolutely! We stand behind all our work with comprehensive warranties. Labor warranties vary by service type, and we also honor all manufacturer warranties on materials and equipment we install.

Do you provide emergency services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services for urgent issues like water leaks, electrical problems, or other situations that pose immediate risks to your property or safety. Emergency service fees may apply for after-hours calls.
