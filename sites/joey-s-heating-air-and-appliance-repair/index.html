Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you repair my broken AC system?+

We offer same-day emergency AC repair service throughout Georgia. In most cases, we can have a technician at your home within 2-4 hours of your call. We keep fully stocked service trucks to handle the majority of repairs on the first visit.

What does AC repair service typically cost?+

Repair costs vary depending on the specific problem, but we always provide upfront pricing before starting any work. Most common repairs range from $150-$600. We never charge hidden fees or surprise you with unexpected costs.

Do you repair all brands of air conditioning systems?+

Yes, our certified technicians are trained to repair all major AC brands including Trane, Carrier, Lennox, Goodman, Rheem, and more. We stock parts for most systems and can quickly order specialty components if needed.

How do I know if I need AC repair or replacement?+

Our technicians will honestly assess your system and explain all your options. Generally, if repair costs exceed 50% of a new system's value, or your AC is over 12 years old with major problems, replacement often makes more financial sense.

Do you offer any warranties on AC repair work?+

Absolutely. All our repair work comes with a comprehensive warranty on both parts and labor. We also offer extended warranty options for additional peace of mind and protection on your investment.
