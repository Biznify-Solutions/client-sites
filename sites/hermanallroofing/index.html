Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to emergency roof repairs?+

We provide 24/7 emergency response and typically arrive within 2-4 hours for urgent situations. Our emergency team carries materials and equipment to provide immediate temporary protection while we schedule permanent repairs. Don't wait when you have a leak - every minute counts in preventing water damage.

Do you provide free estimates for roofing projects?+

Absolutely! We provide detailed, no-obligation estimates for all roofing services at no cost to you. Our estimates include material specifications, timeline, and warranty information so you can make an informed decision. We'll even provide aerial photos and detailed documentation of any issues we find.

What types of roofing materials do you install?+

We install all premium roofing materials including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, slate, and flat roof systems. Our experts will recommend the best material for your home based on your budget, architectural style, and Georgia's climate requirements. All materials come with manufacturer warranties.

Are you licensed and insured for roofing work in Georgia?+

Yes, we maintain full Georgia contractor licensing, general liability insurance, and workers compensation coverage for your complete protection. We provide proof of insurance before starting any project and our licenses are always current. Never hire an uninsured contractor - it puts your property at serious risk.

How long does a typical roof replacement take?+

Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1-3 days depending on size, complexity, and weather conditions. We work efficiently while maintaining strict quality standards and will provide you with a specific timeline during your estimate. Weather delays may extend the schedule, but we'll keep you informed every step of the way.
