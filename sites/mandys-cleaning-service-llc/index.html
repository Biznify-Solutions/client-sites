Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule house cleaning service?+

Most Mobile families benefit from weekly or bi-weekly cleaning depending on household size and lifestyle. Weekly service is ideal for busy families with children or pets, while bi-weekly works well for smaller households or those who maintain tidiness between visits.

What's included in your regular house cleaning?+

Our standard service covers all main living areas including dusting furniture, vacuuming carpets, mopping floors, cleaning bathrooms, and kitchen surfaces. We also empty trash, make beds, and handle detail work like baseboards and light fixtures.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment?+

Yes, we arrive fully equipped with professional-grade supplies and equipment. We use eco-friendly products that are safe for children and pets while delivering superior cleaning results throughout your Mobile home.

Are you licensed and insured for house cleaning in Mobile?+

Absolutely! Mandy's Cleaning Service LLC is fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your protection. Our team members are background-checked professionals who are trained in proper cleaning techniques and safety protocols.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning service?+

We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If any aspect of our cleaning doesn't meet your expectations, simply call us within 24 hours and we'll return to address any concerns at no additional charge.
