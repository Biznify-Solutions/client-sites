How much do your home services cost?

Our pricing varies based on the specific service, materials needed, and complexity of the work. We provide free, detailed estimates for all jobs so you know exactly what to expect. Most routine repairs range from $150-$500, while larger installations may vary. We believe in transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees.

How quickly can you respond to service calls?

For standard appointments, we typically schedule within 24-48 hours. Emergency services are available 24/7 with response times under 2 hours for urgent situations like water leaks, electrical hazards, or heating/cooling failures. We understand that home problems can't wait.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, LC Junk Removal is fully licensed for all home services we provide and carries comprehensive liability insurance. Our technicians hold current certifications and participate in ongoing training. We can provide proof of licensing and insurance upon request.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Absolutely. We provide written warranties on all workmanship, typically ranging from 90 days to 2 years depending on the service. Parts and materials come with manufacturer warranties that we help you navigate. If anything goes wrong, we'll return to fix it at no charge.

Do you handle emergency repairs?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency service for urgent situations that threaten your safety or property. This includes water leaks, electrical problems, heating failures, and security issues. Emergency calls are prioritized and typically receive same-day service.
