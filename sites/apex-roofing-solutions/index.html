Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost?

Roof replacement costs vary based on size, materials, and complexity. Most residential projects in Tuscaloosa range from $8,000 to $25,000. We provide free, detailed estimates so you know exactly what to expect with no hidden fees.

How long does roof installation take?

Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days, depending on size and weather conditions. Repairs are often completed the same day. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your estimate and keep you updated throughout the project.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Apex Roofing Solutions is fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your protection. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. Our workmanship is guaranteed, and we also provide manufacturer warranties on materials. Specific warranty terms depend on the scope of work and materials used.

Do you provide emergency roofing services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services for storm damage, severe leaks, and other urgent roofing issues. We understand that roof problems can't wait, so we prioritize emergency calls to prevent further damage to your home.
