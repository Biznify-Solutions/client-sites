Frequently Asked Questions

How much does roof replacement cost in Tuscaloosa?

Roof replacement costs vary based on size, materials, and complexity. Most residential projects range from $8,000 to $25,000. We provide free detailed estimates that break down all costs with no hidden fees. Financing options are available for qualified customers.

How long does a typical roofing project take?

Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days depending on size and weather conditions. Repairs are often completed the same day. We provide realistic timelines during your estimate and keep you informed throughout the process.

Are you licensed and insured in Alabama?

Yes, Apex Roofing Solutions is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in Alabama. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. All permits and inspections are handled by our team as part of our service.

What warranties do you offer on your work?

We provide comprehensive warranties covering both materials and workmanship. Material warranties range from 20 years to lifetime depending on products chosen. Our workmanship warranty covers 5 years on all installation and repair services.

Do you provide emergency roofing services?

Absolutely. We offer 24/7 emergency response for storm damage, severe leaks, and other urgent roofing issues. Our emergency team can provide temporary protection and permanent repairs to secure your property quickly.
