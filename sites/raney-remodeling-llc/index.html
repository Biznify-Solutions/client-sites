Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle permits and inspections for remodeling projects?+

Absolutely! We handle all permit applications and coordinate inspections with Mobile County officials. Our team knows local building codes inside and out, ensuring your project meets all requirements without delays.

How long do most home remodeling projects take to complete?+

Timeline depends on project scope, but most kitchen remodels take 3-4 weeks and bathroom renovations 2-3 weeks. We provide detailed schedules upfront and keep you updated throughout the entire process.

Are you licensed and insured for work in Mobile County?+

Yes, we're fully licensed, bonded, and insured for all contracting work in Mobile County and surrounding areas. We carry comprehensive liability coverage and workers compensation for your complete protection.

Can you work with my existing budget for home improvements?+

We work with budgets of all sizes and provide multiple options to maximize your investment. During our free consultation, we'll show you exactly what's possible within your budget and suggest phased approaches if needed.

What's included in your free estimate for remodeling work?+

Our comprehensive free estimate includes detailed project scope, materials list, timeline, and fixed pricing with no hidden fees. We also provide design suggestions and answer all your questions during the consultation.
