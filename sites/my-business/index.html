Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to emergency roof repairs?+

We offer 24/7 emergency response throughout Buffalo Grove and surrounding areas. In most cases, we can have a crew on-site within 2-4 hours to assess damage and provide temporary protection. Permanent repairs are typically completed within 24-48 hours depending on weather conditions.

Do you work with insurance companies for storm damage claims?+

Absolutely! We're experienced with all major insurance providers and will work directly with your adjuster to ensure proper coverage. We help document damage, provide detailed estimates, and handle the paperwork to maximize your claim settlement.

What roofing materials do you recommend for Buffalo Grove weather?+

For our harsh winters and severe storms, we recommend architectural shingles from GAF or CertainTeed with proper ice and water shield protection. These materials offer superior wind resistance, ice dam prevention, and longevity that Buffalo Grove homes need.

How long does a typical roof replacement take?+

Most residential roof replacements in Buffalo Grove take 1-3 days depending on size and complexity. We work efficiently while maintaining our high quality standards, and we always protect your belongings and landscaping throughout the process.

What's included in your free roof inspection?+

Our comprehensive inspection covers shingles, flashing, gutters, ventilation, and structural integrity. We provide a detailed written report with photos, priority recommendations, and honest pricing with no high-pressure sales tactics.
