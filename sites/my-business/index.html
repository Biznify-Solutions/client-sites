Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our home services

How much do your home services typically cost?

Service costs vary depending on the specific work needed, materials required, and project complexity. We always provide free, detailed estimates before starting any work. Most routine maintenance and minor repairs range from $150-$500, while larger projects are quoted individually. We're committed to transparent, fair pricing with no hidden fees.

How quickly can you respond to service calls?

For regular service calls, we typically schedule within 24-48 hours of your initial contact. Emergency services are available 24/7, with response times usually within 2-4 hours depending on location and weather conditions. We understand that some home issues can't wait, and we prioritize urgent situations accordingly.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, My Business is fully licensed for home services in ST and carries comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We can provide proof of insurance upon request and ensure all work meets local building codes and industry standards. Your property and our team are fully protected.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Absolutely. We stand behind our workmanship with warranties that vary by service type. Most repairs come with a 90-day guarantee, installations typically include a 1-year warranty, and we honor all manufacturer warranties on parts and materials. If issues arise due to our workmanship, we'll return to make it right at no charge.

Do you provide emergency services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency home services for urgent situations that pose safety risks or could cause property damage. Emergency calls include after-hours response with premium rates. Common emergency situations include major leaks, electrical hazards, security issues, and heating/cooling failures during extreme weather.
