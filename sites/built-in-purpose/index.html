Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical remodeling project take?+

Most kitchen and bathroom remodels take 2-4 weeks depending on scope. Whole home renovations typically require 6-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and stick to our commitments.

Do you handle permits and inspections?+

Absolutely! We manage all permits, inspections, and code compliance for Mobile County. Our team knows local requirements inside and out, ensuring your project passes every inspection the first time.

What's included in your free consultation?+

We provide detailed project assessment, design recommendations, and accurate written estimates at no cost. You'll get a clear roadmap of your project timeline, materials, and investment required.

Can you work with my insurance company?+

Yes, we're experienced with insurance claims for storm damage, water damage, and other covered repairs. We handle documentation, communicate directly with adjusters, and ensure you get maximum coverage.

Do you offer financing options?+

We offer flexible financing solutions to fit any budget, including low-interest payment plans. Many homeowners qualify for same-as-cash options, making your dream remodel affordable today.
