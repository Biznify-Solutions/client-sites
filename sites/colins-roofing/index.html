Frequently Asked Questions

How much does roofing work typically cost?

Roofing costs vary based on the scope of work, materials chosen, and roof size. Minor repairs might cost a few hundred dollars, while complete roof replacements range from $8,000 to $25,000 depending on your home's size and material selection. We provide free, detailed estimates for all projects so you know exactly what to expect with no surprises.

How long does a typical roofing project take?

Most residential roofing projects are completed within 1-3 days, depending on size and weather conditions. Simple repairs can often be finished in a few hours, while complete roof replacements typically take 2-3 days for average-sized homes. We'll provide a clear timeline during your estimate and keep you updated throughout the process.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Colin's Roofing is fully licensed and carries comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We can provide proof of insurance and licensing upon request. This protects both you and our team, giving you peace of mind throughout your roofing project.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind all our work with comprehensive warranties. Our workmanship is guaranteed, and we also provide manufacturer warranties on all materials. Warranty terms vary by project type and materials used, but we'll explain all coverage details before starting your project so you know you're protected.

Do you provide emergency roofing services?

Yes, we understand that roofing emergencies don't wait for business hours. We offer 24/7 emergency services for urgent situations like storm damage, major leaks, or structural issues. Call us at (847) 651-3214 anytime for emergency assistance—we'll prioritize your call and dispatch our team as quickly as possible.
