Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my antique is authentic?+

Our certified appraisers examine construction methods, materials, and historical markers to verify authenticity. We provide detailed documentation and can spot reproductions that others might miss.

What types of antiques do you repair?+

We restore furniture, clocks, china, silverware, paintings, and decorative arts. Our master craftsmen use traditional techniques and period-appropriate materials for authentic restoration.

Do you buy antique collections?+

Yes, we purchase individual pieces and entire estate collections. We offer fair market prices and can handle everything from evaluation to pickup throughout Mobile and surrounding areas.

How long does antique restoration take?+

Restoration timeframes vary based on the piece's condition and complexity. Simple repairs may take 1-2 weeks, while extensive restoration can take 4-8 weeks for the best results.

Can you help with insurance appraisals?+

Absolutely. We provide certified appraisals for insurance, estate planning, and legal purposes. Our detailed reports meet all insurance company requirements and hold up in court.
