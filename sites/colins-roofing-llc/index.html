Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a typical roofing project cost?

Roofing costs vary based on size, materials, and complexity. Most repairs range from $300-$1,500, while full replacements typically cost $8,000-$15,000. We provide detailed written estimates for every project at no charge.

How long does roof installation or repair take?

Most roof repairs can be completed in 1-2 days, while complete installations typically take 2-5 days depending on size and weather conditions. We'll provide a realistic timeline during your estimate and keep you updated throughout the process.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Colins Roofing LLC is fully licensed and carries comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We're happy to provide proof of insurance and licensing upon request for your peace of mind.

What warranty do you offer on your work?

We stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty that covers both materials and workmanship. Warranty terms vary by project type and materials used - we'll explain all warranty details in your written estimate.

Do you handle emergency roofing situations?

Absolutely. We understand that roofing emergencies can't wait. Our emergency response team is available for urgent situations like storm damage, severe leaks, or structural issues that need immediate attention to prevent further damage.
