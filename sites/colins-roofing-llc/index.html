Frequently Asked Questions

How much does roof repair or replacement cost?

Roofing costs vary significantly based on the size of your roof, materials chosen, extent of damage, and complexity of the job. Minor repairs might cost a few hundred dollars, while complete replacements typically range from several thousand to tens of thousands. We provide detailed, written estimates that break down all costs so you can make an informed decision. Every estimate is free with no obligation.

How long does a typical roofing project take?

Most residential roof repairs can be completed in a day or two, while full roof replacements typically take 2-5 days depending on size and complexity. Weather conditions, material availability, and permit requirements can affect timing. We'll provide a realistic timeline during your consultation and keep you informed of any changes throughout the project.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Colin's Roofing LLC is fully licensed and carries comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We're happy to provide copies of our credentials and insurance certificates. This protects both you and our workers, giving you peace of mind that you're working with a legitimate, professional contractor.

What kind of warranty do you offer?

We stand behind our work with solid warranties that vary by service type. Our workmanship warranties typically range from 1-10 years, and we also pass through manufacturer warranties on materials. We'll explain exactly what's covered before starting any work. If issues arise within the warranty period, we'll return to make things right at no cost to you.

Do you provide emergency roofing services?

Absolutely. We understand that roofing emergencies don't wait for business hours. We provide 24/7 emergency services for storm damage, leaks, and other urgent roofing issues. Our emergency team responds quickly to minimize damage to your home and belongings. We'll provide temporary solutions immediately and develop a permanent repair plan once conditions allow.
