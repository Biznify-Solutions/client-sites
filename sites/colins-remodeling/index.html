Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom remodel take?+

Most bathroom remodels take 2-3 weeks from start to finish, depending on the scope of work. We provide a detailed timeline before starting and keep you updated throughout the process. Complex renovations may take slightly longer.

Do you handle permits and inspections?+

Absolutely! We take care of all necessary permits and coordinate inspections with Buffalo Grove officials. Our team knows local building codes inside and out, ensuring your project meets all requirements.

What's included in your remodeling estimate?+

Our detailed estimates include materials, labor, permits, and cleanup. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees or surprise costs. You'll know exactly what to expect before we begin work.

Can you work within my budget?+

Yes! We offer solutions for various budgets and will work with you to prioritize features that matter most. We'll suggest cost-effective alternatives without compromising quality to help you achieve your vision.

How do you minimize disruption during remodeling?+

We use dust barriers, maintain clean work areas, and schedule noisy work during appropriate hours. For kitchen remodels, we can set up temporary cooking areas. Our goal is to make the process as comfortable as possible for your family.
