Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to emergency roof repairs?+

We provide 24/7 emergency response for Theodore homeowners. Most emergency calls receive same-day service, and we always prioritize stopping leaks and preventing further damage. Our rapid response team is always ready to protect your home.

What types of roofing materials do you install?+

We install all major roofing materials including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, and flat roof systems. Our experts will recommend the best material for your home's style, budget, and Theodore's climate conditions. Every material comes with manufacturer warranties.

Do you handle insurance claims for storm damage?+

Absolutely – we work directly with insurance companies to make your claim process smooth and stress-free. Our team documents all damage thoroughly and provides detailed estimates that insurance adjusters trust. We'll fight to get you the coverage you deserve.

How long does a typical roof replacement take?+

Most residential roof replacements in Theodore take 1-3 days depending on size and complexity. Weather conditions and material availability can affect timing, but we always work efficiently to minimize disruption. We'll give you an exact timeline before starting work.

What warranty do you provide on your roofing work?+

We provide comprehensive warranties covering both materials and our workmanship for years of protection. Material warranties come directly from manufacturers, while our labor warranty covers installation quality. You get complete peace of mind with every Wise Builders project.
