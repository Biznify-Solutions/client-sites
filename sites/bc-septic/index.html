Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I pump my septic tank?+

Most septic tanks need pumping every 3-5 years depending on household size and usage. Regular pumping prevents costly backups and extends your system's life. We'll inspect your tank and recommend the right schedule for your specific situation.

What are signs my septic system needs repair?+

Watch for slow drains, sewage odors, wet spots in your yard, or gurgling sounds from drains. These warning signs mean your system needs immediate attention. Call us right away to prevent expensive damage to your property.

How long does septic system installation take?+

Most new septic installations take 2-4 days depending on system size and soil conditions. We handle all permits and inspections to ensure your new system meets Georgia requirements. Weather and site conditions can affect timing.

Do you provide emergency septic services?+

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency septic services throughout Georgia. Septic emergencies can't wait, so we respond quickly to prevent property damage. Call our emergency line anytime for immediate assistance.

How much does septic system service cost?+

Costs vary based on your specific needs, system size, and required services. We provide free estimates for all work and offer competitive pricing with no hidden fees. Most maintenance services are very affordable compared to emergency repairs.
