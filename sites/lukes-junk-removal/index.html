Frequently Asked Questions

How much does junk removal cost?

Our pricing is based on the volume of items removed and the type of materials. We provide upfront, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Most residential jobs range from $100-$600, but we offer free estimates to give you an exact quote for your specific needs.

Can you provide same-day service?

Yes! We often provide same-day junk removal service when our schedule allows. Call us in the morning, and there's a good chance we can have your junk removed the same day. For guaranteed scheduling, we recommend booking 24-48 hours in advance.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Luke's Junk Removal is fully licensed and insured for your protection. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage, so you can have complete peace of mind when we're working on your property.

What items can't you remove?

We can remove most household and commercial items, but we cannot take hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, asbestos, or medical waste. We also don't remove items that require special permits or handling. When you call, we'll discuss any specific items you're unsure about.

Do you recycle or donate items?

Yes! We're committed to environmentally responsible disposal. We donate usable items to local charities and recycle materials whenever possible. Only items that cannot be donated or recycled go to approved disposal facilities, helping reduce landfill waste.
